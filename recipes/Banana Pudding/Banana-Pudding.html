Banana Pudding

Description

The best no bake banana pudding you will ever find (or so I've been told by many).

Ingredients

  • 1 (5 oz) Package Instant Vanilla Pudding Mix
  • 2 cups Cold Milk
  • 1 (14 ounce) Can Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 1 tbps Vanilla Extract
  • 1 (12 oz) Container Frozen Whipped Topping, Thawed
  • 1 (16 oz) Package Vanilla Wafers
  • 14 Bananas, Sliced

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, beat pudding mix and milk 2 minutes. Blend in condensed milk until smooth. Stir in vanilla and fold in whipped topping.
  2. Layer wafers, bananas and pudding mixture in a glass serving bowl. Chill until serving.
